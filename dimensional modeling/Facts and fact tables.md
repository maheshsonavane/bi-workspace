#  Facts and fact tables
> ## Non-additive facts
> facts which cannot be added meaningfully across any
dimensions. <br/>
> `Examples:` 
> - Textual facts
> - Per-unit prices
> - Percentages and ratios
> - Measures of intensity (room temperature)
> - Averages
> - Degenerate Numbers (tx number, order number,etc)
